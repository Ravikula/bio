Small Scale Manufacturing System Automation.




Introduction

The project aimed to automate a small-scale manufacturing system consisting of Processing and Sorting Stations. Using a Siemens S7-1500 PLC and a TP1500 HMI, a fully automated workflow was developed that met rigorous performance and safety requirements. The system handled tasks such as material processing, color-based sorting, and precise control of actuators, making it a robust simulation of real-world manufacturing processes.
