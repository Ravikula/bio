About Me.

I'm currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at RMIT University Melbourne, Australia building upon a solid foundation laid during my Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

My expertise encompasses a wide range of fields including engineering, software development, project management, team leadership, graphic design, and video editing. Rooted in my passion for driving change, I'm deeply engrossed in the realms of Renewable Energy technologies, the Electric Vehicle industry, and harnessing Intelligent Computing (AI) to create a greener, more sustainable future.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I've dedicated a significant portion of my time to Rotaract, amassing over 5 years of invaluable Volunteer experience. This journey has not only enriched me with practical insights but has also led to recognition through a few well-earned awards. Join me as I share my perspectives, aspirations, and endeavors, all aimed at shaping a world where innovation and purpose intertwine harmoniously. Together, let's illuminate the path to a Brighter and Greener tomorrow.
